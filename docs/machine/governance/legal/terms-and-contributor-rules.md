## 1. What TUNES is (and is not)

TUNES is an independent passenger survey of noise experience on ordinary rail journeys, starting with London. It publishes a quieter-journey / noise map and an open methodology with versioned datasets.

TUNES is **not**:

- Operated, approved, or endorsed by Transport for London or any other operator  
- A substitute for Class 1 / engineering-grade acoustic surveys  
- A source of health, safety, or regulatory compliance claims  

Crowd measurements are **survey-grade at best**. Maps must show uncertainty and sample limits. See claim language (ADR-011).

## 3. Contributor rules (recording and uploads)

1. Record only as a passenger in the ordinary course of travel. Do **not** obstruct doors, block aisles, or take unsafe positions for a “better” recording.  
2. Do **not** fabricate, manipulate, or deliberately mislabel journeys or metrics.  
3. Review suggested journey sections before upload. Wrong sections harm privacy and science.  
4. Default upload is **derived features only**. Raw audio stays on your device unless a separate research path (if ever offered) is clearly labelled and consented.  
5. Understand that carriage audio may include other people’s voices; prefer the derived path.  
6. Do not upload free text that identifies other people (names, photos descriptions, medical details about others).  
7. Optional perception answers are your opinions — not medical diagnoses. Do not present them as clinical findings.
