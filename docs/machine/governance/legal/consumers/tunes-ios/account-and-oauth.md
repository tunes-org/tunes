# Account and OAuth (in-app)

**Status:** Draft — counsel review required  
**Version:** 0.1.0-draft  
**Use when:** Sign-in is offered in `tunes-ios`

## Sign-in title

`Sign in to TUNES`

## Body

```
Sign in to upload contributions and manage withdrawals. On iOS we plan to offer Sign in with Apple (and may also support Google). We receive a basic identity from your provider (for example an account ID and email). Accounts are stored with Supabase. We ask only for the scopes we need.

You must be 13 or older. TUNES is not endorsed by TfL.
```
