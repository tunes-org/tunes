# Contributor safety (in-app)

**Status:** Draft  
**Version:** 0.1.0-draft

## Title

`Record safely`

## Body

```
Use TUNES as an ordinary passenger. Do not block doors or aisles, lean into unsafe positions, or distract yourself in ways that reduce awareness of your surroundings.

Do not fabricate journeys. Review section labels before upload.

If recording feels uncomfortable or unsafe, stop and delete the session.
```
