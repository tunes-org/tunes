# Moderation policy

**Status:** Draft — counsel review required  
**Version:** 0.1.0-draft  
**Last-updated:** 2026-07-20

This policy explains how we moderate user-generated content (UGC) on TUNES — including comments, display names, and free-text fields — and when we may refuse or remove contributions that breach programme rules.

---

## 1. Goals

- Keep the community harassment-free and scientifically honest  
- Reduce illegal and harmful content  
- Prevent implied TfL endorsement and unsupported health/risk claims  
- Protect privacy (no doxxing; careful free text)  

---

## 2. What we remove or refuse

| Category | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| Illegal content | Content that appears to be illegal under UK law (including priority illegal harms where the Online Safety Act applies) |
| Abuse / hate / harassment | Targeted harassment, threats, hate speech |
| Doxxing / privacy | Publishing others’ personal details, identifiable incidental speech excerpts |
| Impersonation | Pretending to be TfL, operators, police, or TUNES staff |
| Medical / risk panic | Unsupported health harm or “acoustic risk” claims presented as fact |
| Spam / manipulation | Flooding, fabricated journeys, vote manipulation |
| Unsafe encouragement | Encouraging dangerous recording behaviour |
| Off-topic / low value | At moderators’ discretion when it harms usability |

Scientific disagreement about methods belongs in issues/ADRs — not personal attacks.

---

## 3. How moderation works

1. **Automated signals** (spam heuristics, rate limits, keyword filters) may hide or flag content.  
2. **Human review** for reports, appeals, and significant account actions.  
3. **Pre-moderation** may apply in limited beta (comments unpublished until approved).  
4. Actions: edit (rare), hide, delete, temporary suspension, permanent ban.  

We aim to act on clear illegal content **swiftly** once aware. We do not promise 24/7 coverage for a volunteer-led project; we will state realistic SLAs on the report page.

---

## 4. Appeals

If you believe an action was wrong, email `manglekuo@gmail.com` with the content ID / URL and why. We will reconsider once. Abusive appeals may be ignored.

---

## 5. Transparency

Public terms and this policy describe our approach. We keep internal records of moderation actions as needed for accountability and Online Safety Act duties where applicable.
