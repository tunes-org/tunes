# Code of conduct (outline)

**Status:** Outline only. Full CoC before public community launch.

## Intent

A harassment-free, scientifically honest contributor community. Discourage unsafe measurement behaviour and fabricated data.

## Sketch

- Be respectful; no harassment or discrimination
- Do not encourage risky actions for “better” recordings (e.g. obstructing doors, unsafe carriage positions)
- Do not fabricate or manipulate contributions
- Assume good faith in methodology disagreement; escalate via issues/ADRs
- Privacy: do not share others’ raw audio or identifiable free text from contributions

## Recommendation

Adopt a standard CoC (e.g. Contributor Covenant) with TUNES-specific scientific integrity and safety clauses before Phase 8.
