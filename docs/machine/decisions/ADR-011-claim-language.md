# ADR-011: Claim language (exposure, discomfort, risk)

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-17

## Context

Open question #11. Unsupported health/risk claims are a major programme risk.

## Decision

- Prefer precise acoustic terms: e.g. **LAeq,T**, level ranges, sample counts, tier.
- **Discomfort** only when referring to subjective survey responses.
- Avoid **acoustic risk**, health harm, or regulatory exposure compliance claims for crowd data.
- “Quieter journey” is comparative within the dataset’s limits, with uncertainty shown.

## Consequences

Public map and press copy require review against this ADR; see risk register.
